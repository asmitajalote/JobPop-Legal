Introduction

By voluntarily providing us with Personal Data, as defined below, you are consenting to our use of it in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal data when you:

  • Register for warranty on our website
  • Place an order
  • Respond to a survey
  • Fill out a form

We may collect your name, email address, mailing address, and phone number.

When you create a JobPop account, you provide your email, password, and other personal or business information such as name, date of birth, registered business name, product and customer data.

We collect and store business, product, vendor, and customer data when you use the app. If you share JobPop data with apps like Excel, Outlook, or Adobe, data is exchanged via our API.

When you contact support, your account data may be visible to our support team.

We collect:

  • Contact Information – Used for account setup, support, and communication
  • Device Information – Used for diagnostics and functionality
  • Usage Data – Used for analytics and performance improvement
  • Identifiers – Used for marketing or tracking (if applicable)

Third-Party Services and SDKs

We may use services like Firebase, Google Analytics, or Facebook SDK. These providers handle data according to their own privacy policies. You may manage app permissions via device settings. Contact us at contact@outersnail.com for questions.

Use and Disclosure of Personal Data

Your data may be used to:

  • Improve our products, customer support, and marketing
  • Create/manage accounts, process orders, respond to requests
  • Send communication and notices
  • Provide a personalized experience (e.g. surveys, coupons)
  • Enable features like social sharing or user-to-user interactions
  • Analyze usage, improve services, and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal requirements

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to deliver our Services to you or to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy. For example, we may retain your profile information and information about your business, vendor and customer data during a certain period for as long as you maintain your JobPop account. This provides you with the ability to review your activities over time, track your progress toward personal goals and challenges, and remember your past achievements and milestones (for example, your weight loss goals or other personal achievements).

Because our users may take extended breaks from using scales and may use our Apps on various devices, we do not delete information automatically based on account inactivity or when you uninstall our Apps from one of your devices. However, we provide you with the ability to delete information associated with your account, as follows:

  • Contact info – retained while you have an account
  • Usage data – retained up to 12 months
  • Transaction records – retained up to 7 years

You may delete specific data or your entire account. We will attempt to delete all associated data including third-party authorizations.
