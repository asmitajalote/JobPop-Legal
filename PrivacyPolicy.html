By voluntarily providing us with Personal Data, as defined below, you are consenting to our use of it in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you provide Personal Data through the Services, you acknowledge and agree that such Personal Data may be transferred from your current location to our offices and servers, or those of our third-party contractors. If you would like us to cease using your Personal Data, you may delete your account by following the instructions provided in the “Data Retention and Deletion” section below or exercise the choices described in the “Your Choices and Access to Information” section below.

Information We Collect

When you register for warranty on our website, place an order, respond to a survey and fill out a form, we may collect personal data and other information in connection with the Services, including but not limited to, your name, email address, mailing address, and phone number.

When you create a JobPop account, you provide your email and password, your certain personal and/or business profile information including your name, date of birth, registered business name, product information and customer data. The profile data are necessary factors for determining your access to the App. Your JobPop account allows you to access your customer data through the JobPop mobile app.

When you use the JobPop mobile app, your business, product, vendor and customer data, etc., are collected and stored in your account or retrieved from your account.

When you choose to share your JobPop data with other applications (i.e. MS Excel or Outlook, Adobe Acrobat, etc), we exchange data with them, respectively, via API (Application Programming Interface).

When you contact our customer support, some of your personal, customer, vendor and business data and service data under your JobPop account are available to our support teams.

We collect the following data and types of information through our App:

  • Contact Information (e.g., name, email, phone): Used for account setup, support, and communication.
  • Device Information (e.g., device model, OS version, IP address): Used for diagnostics and app functionality.
  • Usage Data (e.g., app usage patterns): Used for analytics and performance improvement.
  • Identifiers (e.g., advertising ID): Used for marketing or user tracking (if applicable).

Third-Party Services and SDKs

We may use third-party services (e.g., Firebase, Google Analytics, Facebook SDK) for analytics, crash reporting, or marketing purposes. These providers may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

You may manage app permissions such as location or camera access via your device’s Settings. To manage third-party services or data sharing preferences, please visit the relevant sections within the app or contact us at contact@outersnail.com.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to deliver our Services to you or to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy. For example, we may retain your profile information and information about your business, vendor and customer data during a certain period for as long as you maintain your JobPop account. This provides you with the ability to review your activities over time, track your progress toward personal goals and challenges, and remember your past achievements and milestones (for example, your weight loss goals or other personal achievements).

Because our users may take extended breaks from using scales and may use our Apps on various devices, we do not delete information automatically based on account inactivity or when you uninstall our Apps from one of your devices. However, we provide you with the ability to delete information associated with your account, as follows:

  • Contact information: Retained as long as you have an account.
  • Device and usage data: Retained for up to 12 months for analytics.
  • Transaction records: Retained for up to 7 years for compliance and legal reasons.

You may remove a specific activity from your account on a specific date and time.

You may delete your JobPop account and information associated with your account.

  • If you choose to delete your JobPop account, we will make our best effort to delete all information associated with your account, including your profile information, historical data, and third-party authorizations for services connected to your account.
